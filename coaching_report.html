12. Monitoring & Red Flags

Weekly Monitoring

Track These Metrics

  • Resting heart rate (morning, before getting out of bed)
  • Sleep quality and duration
  • Training load (total hours)
  • Intensity distribution (% time in zones)
  • Subjective motivation and energy levels
  • Ability to complete prescribed intervals

Red Flags - Stop Training If:

  • Resting HR elevated 10+ bpm for 3+ consecutive days
  • Unable to complete prescribed intervals at target power
  • Persistent pain (not normal muscle soreness)
  • Sleep disruption lasting 3+ nights
  • Loss of motivation or enjoyment
  • Illness symptoms (fever, persistent cough, etc.)

Action: Take 2-3 days complete rest, then reassess. If symptoms persist beyond 5 days, consult with coach or physician.

Age 51+ Specific Considerations

  • Recovery Time: 48-72 hours between intensity sessions
  • Sleep: 7-9 hours non-negotiable
  • Nutrition: Protein 134-168g/day (1.6-2.0g/kg)
  • Hydration: Monitor urine color (pale yellow = well hydrated)
  • Flexibility: Add 10-15 min stretching post-ride
  • Consistency Over Intensity: Missing workouts worse than slightly lower power

Progress Assessment

Week 8: Reassess FTP (20-min test or ramp test). Adjust zones if FTP has increased.

Week 12: Final FTP test. Celebrate gains and plan next training block.
